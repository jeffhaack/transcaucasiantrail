The
Transcaucasian
Trail

The TCT is a planned trekking route across the Caucasus. It will increase sustainable tourism and bring economic activity to remote areas, raise the profile of the protected areas in the region to ensure their continued conservation, and it will promote the connections between communities, ecosystems, and people, in a place where there is too much focus on the things that divide.

That's us, Paul Stephens and Jeff Haack. In summer 2015 we will be trekking a route from the Black Sea to the Caspian. We'll be joined along the way by fellow enthusiasts, working together to chart the epic trail. This is the first step.

We'll be blogging (coming soon!), tweeting, and photographing along the way, with the aim to build momentum for the project and inform our audience about the protected areas and the cultural and historical sights along the way. When we finish, we will turn all this information into an interactive guide to the trail and produce a written guidebook for others to repeat our journey. In the years to come, we intend to build support for the trail and develop it into a major long-distance hiking trail.
