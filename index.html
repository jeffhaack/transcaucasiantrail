About

The TCT is a planned trekking route across the Caucasus. It will increase sustainable tourism and bring economic activity to remote areas, rais the profile of the protected areas in the region to ensure their continued conservation and promite the connections between communities, ecosystems and people. All in a place where there is much focus on things that divide.

Summer 2015

In the summer of 2015 Paul Stephens and Jeff Haack are trekking and charting the entire route from the Black Sea to the Caspian. We will be producing a website featuring multimedia updates from the trail that will help to build momentum for the project and also inform our audience about the protected areas and the cultural and historical sights along the way. When we get back, we will turn that website into an interactive guide to the trail as well as produce a written guidebook for people who want to repeat our journey.
